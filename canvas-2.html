A book by Sudeep Kaur

So the book is about life, short poem on life, simple problems from the point of view of an outsider, poems of love and of heartbreak.

“I wanted a cover that could just go with the title, Kuch Ankahee Baatein, creating a mystery but at the same time intriguing a passerby into picking up the book” - Sudeep Kaur
Kuch Ankahee Baatein is a compilation of beautiful poems written by the author in a very simple and easy language. The poems represent the thoughts, feelings, emotions, and talent of the author and are sure to take its readers on a very fulfilling and unique journey. Pick up 'Kuch Ankahee Baatein' and discover a beautiful world of poems straight from the author's heart.
